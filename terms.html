2 Description of the service

SongBook is a mobile application for musicians, worship teams, bands, and anyone who sings or plays. It allows users to:

  • Create and manage digital songbooks containing song lyrics and chord annotations for guitar, ukulele, and other string instruments.
  • Add chord positions, capo settings, key signatures, tempo markings, and section labels (verse, chorus, bridge) to songs.
  • Import lyrics from PDF files, plain text files, or by pasting from clipboard.
  • Fetch lyrics automatically via a built-in lyrics search.
  • Share songbooks with other users via 6-character share codes with configurable view or edit permissions.
  • Collaborate on songbooks in real time with other app users.
  • Use songbooks for concerts, church services, campfires, karaoke nights, band rehearsals, camps, and any other setting.

SongBook is available on iOS and Android. Features may vary between platforms.
