🔒 Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters

Last updated: 1 March 2026  ·  Effective immediately

Plain English summary

We collect only what we need to run SongBook. Your private songbooks and chord arrangements stay on your device — they never touch our servers. We never sell your data. Payments are handled entirely by Stripe; we never see your card number.

1 Who we are

SongBook is developed and operated by Marvin Okello ("we", "us", "our"). SongBook is a mobile application that lets you create songbooks with lyrics and chord annotations for guitar, ukulele, and other instruments — perfect for church, concerts, campfires, karaoke, band rehearsals, and more.

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Contact us at okellomarvinkevin@gmail.com.

2 What information we collect

Information you provide

  • Account information: When you register, we collect your email address and password (stored as a bcrypt hash — we cannot read it). You may optionally add a display name.
  • Shared songbook content: Titles, lyrics, chord annotations (guitar chords, ukulele chords, capo positions, keys, tempo), artist names, and section labels for songs in songbooks you choose to share. Private songbooks are stored only on your device and are never sent to our servers.
  • Share codes: When you create a share link, we store the hashed share token and its associated permissions (view or edit). We do not log who views or joins a shared book beyond the direct collaborator relationship.
  • Support messages: If you email us for support, we receive your email address and message content.

Information collected automatically

  • Server logs: Standard request timestamps and IP addresses, retained for up to 30 days for security monitoring. We do not use analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or in-app behaviour tracking.
  • Crash reports: If the app crashes, anonymised error information may be captured to help us fix bugs. No personal content (lyrics, chords) is included.
  • Device information: The app does not collect device identifiers (UDID, IDFA, GAID) or advertising IDs.

We do NOT collect: payment card numbers, bank details, or full financial information (handled entirely by Stripe) · location data · contacts or address book · camera or microphone data · private songbook content (stays on your device).

3 How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account, and authenticate you securely.
  • Sync shared songbooks and chord arrangements between collaborators in real time.
  • Process your Premium subscription payment and activate unlimited access.
  • Respond to support requests.
  • Detect and prevent abuse of the sharing feature.
  • Identify and fix app errors based on crash reports.

We do not use your data for advertising, sell it to third parties, or use it for any purpose beyond operating SongBook.

4 Third-party services

Stripe — Payment processing

Payments are processed by Stripe, Inc. When you purchase premium access, you enter your payment details directly into Stripe's secure payment sheet. We receive only a confirmation that the payment succeeded and a transaction reference ID. We never see, store, or have access to your full card number, expiry date, or CVV.

Stripe's privacy policy: stripe.com/privacy

Lyrics.ovh — Lyrics fetching

When you use the optional "Get Lyrics" feature, the song title and artist name are sent to the public Lyrics.ovh API to retrieve lyrics. No account data is included in this request. See their terms at lyricsovh.docs.apiary.io.

Hosting infrastructure

Our backend and database are hosted on cloud infrastructure in the European Union. Data is processed under applicable data protection law and standard data processing agreements. Private songbooks and chord arrangements are never stored here — they remain on your device only.

5 Data storage and retention

  • Private songbooks (including your personal chord annotations) are stored solely in SQLite on your device and deleted when you uninstall the app or clear app data.
  • Shared songbooks are stored on our servers for as long as your account is active or until you delete the songbook.
  • Account data is retained until you request deletion.
  • Purchase records are retained for 7 years as required by financial record-keeping obligations.
  • Server logs are purged after 30 days.

6 Data security

We protect your data using:

  • HTTPS / TLS encryption for all data in transit between the app and our servers.
  • bcrypt hashing for all stored passwords (we cannot recover your password).
  • JWT tokens with a 7-day expiry for session management.
  • Parameterised database queries to prevent SQL injection.
  • Share codes stored as bcrypt hashes — the raw code is never persisted on our servers.

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. If you discover a security vulnerability, please contact us immediately at okellomarvinkevin@gmail.com.

7 Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate data (you can update your display name directly in the app).
  • Delete your account and associated data by emailing us.
  • Export your data in a portable format on request.
  • Object to processing in certain circumstances.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at okellomarvinkevin@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8 Children's privacy

SongBook is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9 Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we will notify users via email or an in-app notice. Continued use of SongBook after changes take effect constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
