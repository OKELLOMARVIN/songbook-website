FAQ

Common questions

Does the whole group need to pay?

No. Only the person who creates and manages the songbook needs a SongBook account. Members who join via a share code can view and collaborate for free — they don't even need an account for View Only access.

Does it work without an internet connection?

Yes. Your private songbooks are stored on your device and work fully offline. Internet is only required when sharing, syncing edits with collaborators, or fetching lyrics automatically. Perfect for remote camps, outdoor venues, or unreliable connections.

Which instruments does the chord editor support?

The chord editor is instrument-agnostic — you can type any chord name. It includes a quick-pick grid of common guitar and ukulele chords (C, G, Am, Em, F, D, and many more), and you can also type custom voicings like C#m7, Bbadd9, or any notation your band uses.

How many people can join a shared songbook?

Unlimited. Whether it's a 4-person band or a 500-person congregation following along at a concert — the same share code works for everyone. Real-time sync keeps everyone on the same version.

What happens to my free songbooks after 7 days?

Free songbooks become read-only after 7 days — you can still view all your songs and chords, but you won't be able to add or edit songs. Upgrade to Premium to remove the expiry and keep full edit access forever.
